## [Trump says July 4th celebration on National Mall will be a ‘Trump rally’](https://www.ms.now/news/trump-july-4-rally-america-250-national-mall)

_The [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s apparent reframing of the event is the latest example of his politicization of events meant to mark the nation’s 250th anniversary._

Jun. 15, 2026, 12:45 PM EDT
By [Julianne McShane](https://www.ms.now/author/julianne-mcshane)

[President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Donald Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) announced Monday that a July 4th event on the [National Mall](https://www.nps.gov/nama/index.htm) in [Washington](https://dc.gov/), [planned to commemorate the nation’s 250th anniversary,](https://www.ms.now/news/trump-artists-musicians-washington-america-250-anniversary), will be a “[Trump rally](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/).”

In a [Truth Social post](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116753672230328052) Monday morning, [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) said the event, which is slated to be held near the [Lincoln Memorial](https://www.nps.gov/linc/index.htm) and the [Washington Monument](https://www.nps.gov/wamo/index.htm), will be “the most spectacular TRUMP RALLY of them all, a ‘TRIBUTE TO AMERICA.’”

But [Freedom 250](https://freedom250.org/), the public-private [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)-backed partnership [charged with coordinating](https://freedom250.org/celebration/salute-to-america-250-celebration-and-fireworks) events to mark the country’s semiquincentennial, does not describe the event as a [“Trump rally.”](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) Instead, that organization calls it a [“Salute To America 250 Celebration & Fireworks.”](https://freedom250.org/celebration/salute-to-america-celebration-and-fireworks)

“As America marks its 250th anniversary, [Salute to America](https://freedom250.org/celebration/salute-to-america-celebration-and-fireworks) will stand as a defining celebration of the people, principles, and patriotic spirit that make the United States the greatest nation in the world,” [Freedom 250](https://freedom250.org/)’s event description says, which does not mention the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) by name.

[Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/)’s apparent reframing of the July 4th event marked the latest example of his politicization of events meant to celebrate the nation’s 250th anniversary.

After several musical artists dropped out of the upcoming [“Great American State Fair”](https://freedom250.org/celebration/the-great-american-state-fair) event series over its ties to [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/), the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) said he would join the opening ceremony later this month and lambasted the artists who canceled their participation.

On Sunday, the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) hosted a [UFC event](https://www.ms.now/opinion/white-house-ufc-match-trump-birthday) on the [South Lawn](https://www.whitehouse.gov/), which officials said was meant to be part of the 250th anniversary celebrations, but also coincided with the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s 80th birthday.

The July 4th event, [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) announced on Truth Social, will include a fireworks display and musical performances, including from [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/)’s own playlist, he said.

“There will be incredible Flyovers and Airshows featuring our Top Military Pilots and Equipment, and I will deliver keynote remarks that you will not want to miss,” the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) wrote.
