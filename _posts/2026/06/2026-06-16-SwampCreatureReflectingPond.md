## [Reflecting Pool’s algae bloom is Trump metaphor come to life](https://www.ms.now/opinion/trump-reflecting-pool-algae-corruption-incompetence-national-mall)

_An algae bloom has turned the [Reflecting Pool](https://www.nps.gov/places/000/reflecting-pool.htm) green after a multimillion-dollar project [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) claimed would make it less “ugly” and “filthy.”_

Jun. 15, 2026, 4:48 PM EDT
By Ja'han Jones

[Donald Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) has inadvertently created a physical swamp that serves as a potent metaphor for his [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/)’s corruption and incompetence.

The man who popularized the phrase [“drain the swamp”](https://www.ms.now/rachel-maddow-show/maddowblog/drain-the-swamp-trump-punchline-corruption) as a vow to rid politics of corruption has failed to live up to his word. Meanwhile, his failed attempt to beautify the [Reflecting Pool](https://www.nps.gov/places/000/reflecting-pool.htm) in [Washington, D.C.](https://dc.gov/), has become the perfect epitome of the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s ability to deliver funds to his friends at taxpayers’ expense.

As my colleague Steve Benen wrote in April, [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) called the [Reflecting Pool](https://www.nps.gov/places/000/reflecting-pool.htm) “dirty” and “filthy” en route to awarding [one of his associates a no-bid contract](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/may/09/trump-no-bid-contract-lincoln-memorial-reflecting-pool) to beautify it. But just days after the job was completed, at a cost of more than \$14 million, algae is in full bloom throughout the pool, giving it the look of a [Florida](https://www.myflorida.gov/) [swamp](https://www.nps.gov/ever/index.htm).

Experts [warned The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2026/05/31/us/trump-reflecting-pool-problems.html?smid=tw-share) last month that an algae bloom was likely if the [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/) didn’t take proper steps to improve the pool’s pipe and filter system. And those concerns have been realized despite an expensive effort to mitigate the problem, as you can tell from a [DC News Now report featuring angry D.C. visitors](https://www.dcnewsnow.com/news/local-news/washington-dc/algae-returns-to-reflecting-pool-days-after-completion-of-14-2-million-renovation/).

Pro-[Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) influencer Greg Stinchfield drew jeers on social media for claiming without evidence the algae was a result of liberal “sabotage” and “vandalism.”

The root issues with this swampy scenario are more likely [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/)’s ignorance, haste and misuse of taxpayer funds. Irony may be dead, but the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s commitment to boondoggles that bilk the American taxpayer while enriching himself and his allies is very much alive.
