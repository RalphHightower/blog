## [Kash Patel called to White House on heels of continued questions over his behavior](https://www.ms.now/news/kash-patel-called-to-white-house-on-heels-of-continued-questions-over-his-behavior)

_The embattled [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) director was set to travel to [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) in the [Bureau](https://www.fbi.gov/)'s jet to see his [girlfriend](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexis-wilkins-87712549/) perform at a music festival when he was called in, sources tell [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/)._

Jul. 10, 2026, 6:13 PM EDT
By [Carol Leonnig](https://www.ms.now/author/carol-leonnig), [Jacqueline Alemany](https://www.ms.now/author/jacqueline-alemany), [Ken Dilanian](https://www.ms.now/author/ken-dilanian-2), [Vaughn Hillyard](https://www.ms.now/author/vaughn-hillyard-2) and [Jake Traylor](https://www.ms.now/author/jake-traylor)

[FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) [Director Kash Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) abruptly cancelled a planned flight Friday to see his girlfriend in [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) this weekend, after top [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/) officials frustrated with [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) summoned him to the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/), according to two people with knowledge of the change.

The precise reasons that [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s political bosses demanded he cancel his trip and report to the [West Wing](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) are unclear, but several people said top [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) deputies were disturbed by a range of actions by [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel). Some found it confounding that the [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) director was leaving town amid the recent revival of the war with [Iran](https://iran.ir/) and alleged threats against the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s life, according to a person familiar with efforts to help [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) rebuild trust with the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/). For this article, people spoke on the condition of anonymity to discuss a sensitive internal matter.

Others cited two unforced errors by [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) that created bad optics for the [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/), the first being [his early morning tweet bashing MS NOW](https://x.com/kash_patel/status/2075544869267706152?s=46) for its coverage of his high-flying lifestyle, they said, in which [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) boasted: “my jet ski is gold plated…dumbass.”

The second misstep, the people said, was that extensive reports by [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/) and other news outlets about taxpayers footing the bill for [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s globe-trotting ultimately spurred formal questions from [Senate Judiciary](https://www.judiciary.senate.gov/) [Chairman](https://www.judiciary.senate.gov/about/chair) [Chuck Grassley (T-IA)](https://www.grassley.senate.gov/), a critical ally of [President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/)’s.

On Thursday, [MS NOW reported exclusively](https://www.ms.now/news/house-democrats-want-kash-patel-to-answer-for-his-spending-but-this-time-theyre-not-alone) that [Grassley](https://www.grassley.senate.gov/) had first asked [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) in a May letter about his use of the [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) jet and his decision to [purchase luxury armored BMWs](https://www.ms.now/news/kash-patels-new-ride-of-choice-an-armored-luxury-bmw) so he could travel more discreetly in the [Washington, DC](https://dc.gov/) area.  In a separate letter, [Congressional](https://www.congress.gov/) [Democrats](https://www.democrats.org/) said they had received information that [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) was demanding that [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) staff arrange special perks on his official trips, including a jet ski excursion and a helicopter tour.  The [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) has disputed that, and said [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) had complied with federal rules pertaining to his travel.

[White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) spokesperson Steven Cheung issued a statement without responding to specific questions about [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s cancelled trip or him being summoned to the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/).

“Under [President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) and [Director Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s leadership at the [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/), crime across the country has plummeted to the lowest level in more than 100 years and many high profile criminals have been put behind bars,” he said. “[Director Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) remains a critical player on the [Administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s law and order team.”

When asked to clarify why [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) was at the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) on Friday, a [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) official said, “The idea that he was ‘summoned to the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’ due to ‘frustration’ is totally inaccurate. He was here for unrelated meetings.”

An [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) spokesman declined to comment for this article.

[Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) cancelled his flight to [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) just as he was preparing to leave on Friday morning, according to three people briefed on his move. But the trip to [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) was already stirring anger and controversy inside the [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/), multiple current and former law enforcement officials told [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/). He was planning to attend his girlfriend [Alexis Wilkins](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexis-wilkins-87712549/)’ performance Saturday at a [country music festival held in the parking lot of a major Chicago stadium](https://www.bandsintown.com/f/202109?came_from=250), the three people said. [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s staff had arranged for the director to make an office visit Friday to the [Chicago field office](https://www.fbi.gov/contact-us/field-offices/chicago) to coincide with his trip, according to two other people with knowledge of the director’s schedule.

Several [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) agents complained internally that this office visit was belatedly-added cover to justify [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) flying the director’s jet — which is estimated to cost tens of thousands of dollars for such domestic trips — to enjoy a weekend in [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) with his girlfriend, the people said.

“[Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) was coming (to [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/)) today for a fake office visit for his girlfriend’s country concert this weekend,” one of the sources briefed on the trip said.  The source noted [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) “cancelled the trip while on the tarmac at Andrews” and was “summoned to the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) immediately,” adding that it was “apparent panic” and “believed to be in response to his morning tweet today.”

Reports on [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s personal travel have drawn a flurry of [Democratic](https://www.democrats.org/) [lawmakers](https://www.congress.gov/)’ questions and even [President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/)’s ire in some cases, most notably when he used the director’s jet to fly to [Milan](https://www.comune.milano.it/) to see the [U.S. men’s ice hockey team](https://teamusa.usahockey.com/2026olympicmenroster) [finals](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/results/iho/m/team6-------------/fnl-/000100--/team-playbyplay) in the [Olympics](https://www.olympics.com/), a trip that former [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) officials estimate cost at least $1 million when accounting for both the flight costs and expenses for his security detail, pilots and hotel bills. [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s office said at the time that he had multiple security and diplomatic meetings in [Italy](https://www.governo.it/) that made this an official visit. [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) had tweeted the previous year, however, in July 2025, of his plans to attend the [men’s hockey](https://teamusa.usahockey.com/2026olympicmenroster) competition.

[Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) shared his disappointment directly with [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel), sources told [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/) at the time, complaining about the bad look the director had created for the [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/) and the [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) when he was captured on [video chugging beer in the hockey team’s locker room](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/NmR5QMDeLm0).

[Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) also drew scrutiny from conservative supporters of [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) when [he used the director’s jet to travel to Penn State to watch Wilkins perform the national anthem](https://onwardstate.com/2025/10/31/fbi-director-kash-patel-accused-of-using-60m-government-jet-for-personal-trip-to-penn-state/). [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) and his office at the time denied he misused [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) resources, and noted that the [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) director is required to fly on the government jet when leaving the area, including on personal trips. Former [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/) officials acknowledged that is true, but said that past directors were judicious about personal trips.

The [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) event where [Wilkins](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexis-wilkins-87712549/) is scheduled to perform Saturday is the [Windy City Smokeout](https://www.windycitysmokeout.com/), which is promoted as an annual summer festival in the heart of [Chicago](https://www.chicago.gov/) featuring several days of country music acts, barbecue and craft beer.  [Wilkins](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexis-wilkins-87712549/) is listed on the event’s website as being scheduled to perform on Saturday.

A source familiar with efforts to help [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) repair his standing in the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) told [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/) there have been multiple interventions by people who like [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) and have been trying to help him in his position as director.

“With everything going on in the war, how are you not outside the [Oval Office](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) ready to go into the [Situation Room](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)?” the source said.

According to two people who communicate frequently with the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/), [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) and some senior deputies, including [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) chief of staff [Susie Wiles](https://www.linkedin.com/in/susiewiles/), have been irritated by [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) repeatedly drawing negative news coverage that reflects poorly on the [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/) and draws attention away from what they consider its accomplishments.

[Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel)’s travel, especially his trip to the [Olympics](https://www.olympics.com/) and his office’s claims to [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/) that it was for official business, became the butt of a series of late-night host jokes and even featured actor Aziz Ansari mockingly playing [Patel](https://www.fbi.gov/about/leadership-and-structure/director-patel) in a cold open for [Saturday Night Live](https://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live).
