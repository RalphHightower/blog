## FOIA Requests

### FOIA Email to Attorney General Wilson

Subject: FOIA Request for Existing Records on Adjudicated Election-Fraud Cases

Pursuant to the South Carolina Freedom of Information Act, I request copies of existing records held by the Attorney General’s Office documenting adjudicated election-fraud cases in South Carolina.

Please provide the following for each eveb calendar year from 2010 through 2024 for Federal elections only:

• Annual count of adjudicated election-fraud cases, including criminal convictions, plea agreements, civil findings, and overturned elections.
• Statutory category for each case, including illegal voting, double voting, absentee ballot fraud, registration fraud, or other applicable category.
• Number of referrals received from county election offices or SLED.

This request is limited to records already maintained by your office. I am not requesting the creation of new reports, summaries, or compilations.

Electronic format is preferred.

Please provide a cost estimate before processing, as required.

Thank you.

### SC Votes

#### SCVotes FOIA Form: Description of Records Requested

I request copies of existing records documenting statewide election activity for each even calendar year from 2010 through 2024 for Federal elections only.

Specifically:

• Total ballots cast statewide, including in-person, absentee, early, and provisional ballots.
• Any existing referral records documenting cases forwarded to the Attorney General’s Office, SLED, or county solicitors for potential election-related violations.

This request is limited to records already maintained by the South Carolina Election Commission. I am not requesting the creation of new reports, summaries, or compilations.

Electronic format is preferred.

Please provide a cost estimate before processing.

#### SCVotes FOIA Form: Intended Use of the Data

To calculate year-by-year election statistics using existing state records.
