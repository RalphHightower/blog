## [Officials say Trump switched out new Air Force One over security concerns](https://www.ms.now/news/officials-say-trump-switched-out-new-air-force-one-over-security-concerns)

_Rising hostilities with [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) required [President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Donald Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) to shift to his original, more advanced [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)ial aircraft_

Jul. 8, 2026, 9:53 PM EDT
By [Carol Leonnig](https://www.ms.now/author/carol-leonnig), [Priya Sridhar](https://www.ms.now/author/priya-sridhar) and [Ken Dilanian](https://www.ms.now/author/ken-dilanian-2)

[President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Donald Trump](https://www.ms.now/opinion/politicized-intelligence-dni-firings-spy-list-panetta) swapped out his new [Qatari-gifted Air Force One](https://www.ms.now/news/trump-new-air-force-one-qatar-jet) for his flight from the [NATO summit](https://www.ms.now/opinion/trump-nato-summit-rutte-erdogan) to the [United Kingdom](https://www.gov.uk/) over concerns that the gifted aircraft lacks the secure communications systems and military defenses needed to safely manage a rapidly [escalating conflict with Iran](https://www.ms.now/liveblog/iran-war-news-today-july-8-2026), according to two former national security officials familiar with the matter.

Instead, [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) flew from [Ankara](https://www.ankara.gov.tr/) to a U.S. base in the [United Kingdom](https://www.gov.uk/) in his original [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/). It was during that flight that [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) authorized additional strikes against [Iran](https://irangov.ir/), following through on a threat he had made earlier that day to end the on-again, off-again ceasefire.

[Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/)’s earlier choice to use the gifted [Qatari](https://hukoomi.gov.qa/) jet as a primary [presidential](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) aircraft has been controversial because the gifted plane lacks key military defenses of the original [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/), including the ability to refuel mid-air and missile-defense systems, according to a law enforcement official briefed on its capabilities.

The original [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/) — a [Boeing](https://www.boeing.com/) VC-25A from the early 1990s — is a unique military plane outfitted with these capabilities.

The main concern Wednesday was the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)’s need to use the command-and-control functions of the original [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/), according to two other former security officials. Those systems turn the plane into a “flying situation room,” allowing the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) to receive classified intelligence briefings, communicate securely and give orders to military officers around the world.

The [Qatari](https://hukoomi.gov.qa/) [Boeing](https://www.boeing.com/) VC-25B — originally manufactured as a passenger jet in 2012 — does not have some of the original [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/)’s equipment, and some of the jet’s capabilities have not been fully tested, the sources added.

“This plane was built for aesthetics, not mission,” one former national security official said. “We know about its paint, its leather seats. But we don’t know its capabilities.”

A U.S. Air Force spokesperson declined to state why [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) shifted from the [Qatari](https://hukoomi.gov.qa/) plane to the original [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/) jet for his flight to the [U.K.](https://www.gov.uk/), but said there was no concern about the jet’s abilities.

“The [Air Force](https://www.af.mil/) is confident the VC-25B Bridge Aircraft is safe, secure and equipped with the most advanced technologies necessary to meet the requirements of the [presidential](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) mission,” the spokesperson said.

Asked during his closing press conference in [Ankara](https://www.ankara.gov.tr/) on Wednesday whether the plane switch had to do with security concerns, [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) acknowledged he is “No. 1 on the kill list for [Iran](https://irangov.ir/).” He also said the change was to allow the new plane to be seen by U.S. troops at a [U.K.](https://www.gov.uk/) military base.

The [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) added that he would be flying from the [U.K.](https://www.gov.uk/) back to the U.S. on the new [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/).

The [Secret Service](https://www.secretservice.gov/) and [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) declined to comment Wednesday evening. [Boeing](https://www.boeing.com/) did not immediately respond to requests for comment.

[Rep.](https://www.house.gov/) [Jim Himes (D-CT4)](https://himes.house.gov/), the top [Democrat](https://www.democrats.org/) on the [House Intelligence Committee](https://intelligence.house.gov/), said he believes the [Qatari](https://hukoomi.gov.qa/) jet given to [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) does not have the same protections as the previous [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/).

The new [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/), a VC25-B, was given to the [Air Force](https://www.af.mil/) to outfit for the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/)ial mission last May.

National security officials say it typically takes years, as well as billions of dollars, to make an [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/) aircraft safe and secure — time and money that was not spent here.

Both the [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) and the [Air Force](https://www.af.mil/) have previously said “no shortcuts were taken” with the new [Air Force One](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104588/vc-25-air-force-one/), adding that the new plane is equipped with advanced technology and that critical mission requirements were prioritized over aesthetics.

David Rohde and Vaughn Hillyard contributed to this story.
