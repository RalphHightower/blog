## [Trump urges other nations to send warships to the Mideast](https://www.ms.now/news/u-s-trump-warships-iran-mideast)

_The U.S. on Friday pummeled Kharg Island, [Iran](https://irangov.ir/)’s major oil export terminal, in an apparent effort to pressure [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) to open the Strait of Hormuz._

Mar. 14, 2026, 6:05 PM EDT
By [Clarissa-Jan Lim](https://www.ms.now/author/clarissa-jan-lim)

[President](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) [Donald Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) is asking other countries to send warships to the Middle East to help reopen the Strait of Hormuz, the vital gateway off the coast of [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) for the world’s oil supply.

“Hopefully [China](https://www.gov.cn/), [France](https://www.info.gouv.fr/), [Japan](https://www.japan.go.jp/), [South Korea](https://www.mois.go.kr/), the [UK](https://www.gov.uk/), and others, that are affected by this artificial constraint, will send Ships to the area so that the Hormuz Strait will no longer be a threat by a Nation that has been totally decapitated,” [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) wrote Saturday [in a post](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116227904143399817) on Truth Social as the U.S. prepared to send thousands of additional troops to the region.

“In the meantime,” the [president](https://www.whitehouse.gov/) vowed, “the United States will be bombing the hell out of the shoreline, and continually shooting [Iranian](https://irangov.ir/) Boats and Ships out of the water.”

The U.S. embassy in [Baghdad](https://amanatbaghdad.gov.iq/), meanwhile, urged all American citizens to “leave Iraq immediately,” [warning](https://x.com/USEmbBaghdad/status/2032865661869797409) that [Iran](https://irangov.ir/)-backed militias have carried out numerous attacks on U.S. citizens and targets throughout [Iraq](https://ur.gov.iq/).

In an exclusive interview with [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/)’s Ayman Mohyeldin on Saturday, [Iranian](https://irangov.ir/) [Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi](http://www.mfa.gov.ir/) railed against the [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) [administration](https://www.whitehouse.gov/administration/), saying, “We didn’t start this war. It was an unprovoked, unwarranted, illegal act of aggression against us, and we are only defending ourselves, and we continue to defend ourselves as much as it takes and as long as it takes in order to end this war in a way that it won’t be repeated in the future.”

He also said there was “no problem” with [Iran](https://irangov.ir/)’s new supreme leader, [‌Mojtaba ⁠Khamenei](https://www.leader.ir/), who [Defense](https://www.defense.gov/) [Secretary Hegseth](https://www.defense.gov/About/Biographies/Biography/Article/4040890/hon-pete-hegseth/) said on Friday was “wounded and likely disfigured.”

The U.S. struck more than 90 military targets Friday on Kharg Island, [Iran](https://irangov.ir/)’s major oil export terminal, in what appeared to be an effort to pressure [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) to open the strait.

[Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) first announced the strike in a [Truth Social post](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116224324444349237) post Friday night, saying the island’s oil infrastructure was left intact. But he threatened to strike its oil facilities “should [Iran](https://irangov.ir/), or anyone else, do anything to interfere with the Free and Safe Passage of Ships through the Strait of Hormuz.”

The U.S. hit naval mine storage facilities and missile storage bunkers on the island, among other military sites, according to [U.S. Central Command](https://x.com/CENTCOM/status/2032777791247155482).

Roughly 90% of [Iran](https://irangov.ir/)’s oil is exported from Kharg Island. The strike has not appeared to deter [Iran](https://irangov.ir/), however. The [Iranian Revolutionary Guard](https://sepahnews.ir/) said its Navy remained in control of the Strait of Hormuz and reiterated that vessels “belonging to aggressors and their allies” are barred from the waterway, [The New York Times reported](https://www.nytimes.com/live/2026/03/14/world/iran-war-trump-oil-israel/1a1f09ed-5b38-5b88-82e6-68ac9b6aa382?smid=url-share), citing [Iranian](https://irangov.ir/) media.

“Any attempt to move or transit will be targeted,” it added.

[Reuters also reported](https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/trump-threatens-strike-irans-kharg-island-oil-network-if-shipping-lanes-remain-2026-03-14/) that the [IRGC](https://sepahnews.ir/) claimed it has a right to target U.S. interests in the [United Arab Emirates]() in self-defense and warned civilians to evacuate ports, docks, and U.S. military shelters.

The helipad at the [U.S. embassy in Baghdad](https://iq.usembassy.gov/) was struck Friday, according to [The Associated Press](https://apnews.com/article/iran-iraq-us-trump-march-14-2026-oil-prices-a2399398b4c590995b814d7640362a11?taid=69b4df03567074000195f5b3&), though no party has taken responsibility for it.

In the interview with [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/), [Araghchi](http://www.mfa.gov.ir/) denied that [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) was targeting civilian infrastructure in neighboring countries.

“What we are doing in as an act of self defense is to targeting American bases, American installations, American assets and American interests, which are unfortunately located in the territory of our neighbors,” he said, adding, “So what we are doing is only the principle of an eye for an eye.”

The war with [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) is entering its third week with no apparent end in sight. More than 2,000 people have died in the Middle East, with death tolls highest in [Iran](https://irangov.ir/) and [Lebanon](http://www.pcm.gov.lb/), where [Israel](https://www.gov.il/)’s attacks on Hezbollah are leading to what human rights organizations say is a [humanitarian crisis](https://www.mercycorps.org/press-room/releases/lebanon-war-displacement-humanitarian-aid-funding).

The [Israeli](https://www.gov.il/) military [said](https://x.com/IDF/status/2032858899355673007) Saturday that it “eliminated” Abdollah Jalali-Nasab and Amir Shariat, two senior [Iranian](https://irangov.ir/) intelligence officials who were close to regime leadership.

Oil prices hover near all-time highs as the Strait of Hormuz remains closed to shipping vessels. [Trump](https://www.donaldjtrump.com/) said Friday that the [U.S. Navy](https://www.navy.mil/) will start escorting tankers through the strait “very soon.”

The U.S. is sending up to 5,000 additional service members, including the [31st Marine Expeditionary Unit](https://www.31stmeu.marines.mil/), and several additional ships to the Arabian Sea, a U.S. official with knowledge of the matter told [MS NOW](https://www.ms.now/).
