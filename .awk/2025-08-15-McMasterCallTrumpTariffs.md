## Phone Script for Governor McMaster to Call Trump About Tariffs 

Hi, this is Ralph Hightower from Chapin. I’m calling to ask Governor McMaster to use his influence with President Trump to reverse the new import tariffs.

Since President Trump appointed then–Lt. Governor McMaster to the governorship, I assume they still have a direct line. I hope Governor McMaster will pick up the phone and urge the President to reconsider these tariffs before they do lasting damage.

Economists across the spectrum are sounding the alarm: Trump’s tariffs are driving up prices while slowing growth—a toxic mix known as stagflation. We haven’t seen that since the 1970s, and the Federal Reserve doesn’t have a playbook to fix it. Their tools can fight a recession or inflation—but not both at once.

The average tariff rate is now 18%, the highest since 1934. Businesses are passing those costs on to consumers, and some are scaling back hiring to cope. That’s not just bad policy—it’s bad math.

Trump’s “America First” is starting to look like “America Alone.” We’re isolating ourselves economically while our allies strike deals elsewhere. South Carolina’s exporters and consumers will be left holding the bag.

Governor McMaster, please call the President. Let him know these tariffs aren’t just unpopular—they’re economically reckless.
