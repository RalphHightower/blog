📘 Florida Man
(noun)  

1. A stock figure in American internet folklore representing a man from Florida whose actions, often drawn from local news reports, are portrayed as bizarre, reckless, or absurd.  

2. A meme format beginning with “Florida Man…” used to highlight chaotic or improbable behavior, typically framed as humorous and emblematic of the state’s cultural stereotypes.  

Example: “Florida Man attempts to trade an alligator for beer.”

---

📘 Florida Woman
(noun)  

1. A counterpart to Florida Man, representing a woman from Florida whose actions, as reported in local news, are depicted as unusual, unpredictable, or outlandish.  

2. A meme construction beginning with “Florida Woman…” used to underscore incidents that mirror the eccentricity associated with the broader Florida‑based folklore tradition.  

Example: “Florida Woman steals a swan boat to ‘rescue’ it from the lake.”

---

📘 Usage Note
The terms Florida Man and Florida Woman originated from the visibility of Florida’s public records and the frequency of unusual incidents reported by local news outlets. Both expressions function as shorthand for a genre of American folklore centered on chaotic, improbable, or comedic behavior.  

While often humorous, the labels can also be used in commentary on public life, civic behavior, or political culture when individuals become emblematic of the meme’s themes.
